THE “ALWAYS GARDEN”

URBAN LEGEND #42814

Now THATS-a-spicy PURGATORY! Usually found in strip malls and acid flashbacks, The Always Garden looks like a medium-budget Italian restaurant, but if you walk inside to grab a handful of parmesan you’ll discover you’re now forever lost in the labrynthine digestive tract of an ancient unknowable god. Come for the damp caesar salad, stay because escape is literally impossible. As the commercial says “The Always Garden: When you’re here, YOU’RE HERE.”
